Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

WorkπServ ("we", "us", "the platform") is a freelance marketplace powered by the Pi Network ecosystem, where members of the Pi community ("Pioneers") can buy and sell services using Pi, protected by an Escrow system. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Pi-exclusive by design. WorkπServ uses Pi Network authentication only and processes transactions in Pi only. We never ask for, store, or have access to your 24-word secret passphrase or private keys.

1. Information we collect

  • Pi authentication data: when you sign in through the Pi SDK, we receive your Pi username and a unique account identifier provided by Pi Network.
  • Profile information: details you choose to add, such as display name, bio, and profile image.
  • Pi public wallet address: the public Stellar-format address (starting with "G") that you enter yourself so freelancers can receive released funds. We never collect private keys or secret phrases.
  • Service and order data: service listings you publish, orders, and Escrow status.
  • Messages: content you exchange with other users through the platform's messaging feature.
  • Images: files you upload (e.g., service or profile images), stored via our image provider.
  • Technical data: limited technical information needed to operate and secure the service.

2. How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and operate your account.
  • To publish and display your service listings and to facilitate orders.
  • To process payments in Pi and manage Escrow holds and releases.
  • To enable messaging between buyers and sellers.
  • To moderate content, prevent fraud and abuse, and keep the marketplace safe.
  • To respond to your requests and provide support.

3. Payments, Pi Network and Escrow

All transactions on WorkπServ are conducted in Pi through the Pi Network platform. When a buyer pays for a service, the funds are held in Escrow and released to the freelancer's provided public wallet address only after the buyer confirms delivery. You are solely responsible for the accuracy of the public wallet address you enter; funds sent to an incorrect address cannot be recovered by WorkπServ.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate the service, including with infrastructure providers that host our database, store uploaded images, and run our servers, and with Pi Network for authentication and payments. Other users can see the information you make public, such as your username, profile, service listings, and the messages you send to them.

5. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us.

6. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the email below.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Please protect your Pi account and never share your secret passphrase with anyone, including WorkπServ.

8. Children

WorkπServ is not directed to individuals under the age required to hold a Pi Network account in their jurisdiction, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any question about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at alybentaher@gmail.com.
